## Pattern

### Breakfast

* 2 x Complex Carb  
* 1 x Milk  
* Breakfast Misc  

### Mid-morning Snack

* 1 x Fruit  
* 1 x Milk  

### Lunch

* 2 x Complex Carb  
* 1 x Protein  
* 1-3 x Veg  
* 2 x Fat

### Early Afternoon Snack  

* 2 x Fruit  

### Late Afternoon Snack

* 2 x Carb
* 1 x Protein  
* 1 x Fat  

### Dinner

* 2 x Complex Carb  
* 1.5 x Protein  
* 2-4 x Veg

----------------------------------
